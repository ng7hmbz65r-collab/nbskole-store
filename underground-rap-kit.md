## THE 5 OPENERS THAT WIN (steal these structures)

1. **THE STOP**
   "Okay, stop it, stop it, stop it." — @logic (4.4M)
   Use when you're about to drop a hard truth. Halt scroll, then hit.

2. **THE CONFESSION**
   "I'm 54 years old, and I'm not gonna lie to you." — @hattie_willoughby7 (1.4M)
   Age + vulnerability = instant trust. Rappers: "I'm 23 and I ain't gonna lie to you…"

3. **THE THEORY**
   "So I've been using this thing called the let them theory." — @melrobbins (2.2M)
   Name a concept, then teach it. "So I've been using this thing called the quiet artist…"

4. **THE ORDER**
   "Get out. I order you." — @ariel.fitz (906K)
   Command energy. Short. Violent. Memorable.

5. **THE COMMITMENT**
   "I am completely committed to letting people miss out on me." — @_joshuarosa (669K)
   Bold stance = shares. Pick a side, don't hedge.

## THE FORMULA (from 55M likes of data)
- Hook in first 2 seconds. No intro, no "what's up."
- Specific > vague. "A 39 year old man" beats "some people."
- Accusatory > poetic for reach. Poetic > accusatory for saves.
- Name a feeling your audience is scared to say out loud.
