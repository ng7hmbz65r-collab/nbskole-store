# NBSKOLE — VIRAL HOOKS PACK (Vol. 1)
### 40 fire lines for artists, outsiders & the ones who didn't mute you
*Curated by Daniel Kole (nbskole) from deep research into creators like @forevertherapy, @daysbewilin, iamwillin, accejayyy, okaineofficial.*

---

## THE 40 (copy any of these — they're yours)

1. Stop begging for a seat at a table you were meant to build.
2. You keep auditioning for people who already saw the whole show and clapped for someone else.
3. The people who left on read taught you more than the ones who stayed ever did.
4. You call it loyalty. It costs you everything and returns nothing. Name it again.
5. You were never too much. You were just around people running on empty.
6. You didn't lose them. You stopped funding a habit that was never yours to feed.
7. The version of you they miss is the one that never told them no.
8. You keep lighting yourself on fire to keep people warm who wouldn't spit on you if you burned.
9. Being chosen last isn't humility. It's a bill you keep paying for a service you never got.
10. You don't have trust issues. You have memory.
11. The silence after you stopped explaining yourself is the loudest peace you'll ever hear.
12. You outgrew the room. That's why it suddenly feels so small.
13. You mistook exhaustion for love because no one ever showed you the difference.
14. Comparison is just you handing strangers the pen to grade a test they never took.
15. Your father's silence built the man who now can't sit still in a quiet house.
16. Half of your ambition is just a boy trying to make a phone call that will never be answered.
17. Envy is you admitting out loud what you were too scared to chase in private.
18. You don't want their life. You want proof that yours could have been that too.
19. Nobody stole your shot. You kept handing it to people who flinched.
20. You keep restarting the race so no one can say you actually lost it.
21. An artist who waits to feel ready dies with the whole album still inside him.
22. They'll call it luck the day after they watched you do it alone for years.
23. The song you're scared to release is the only one that was ever really yours.
24. You keep making music for the people who muted you. Make it for the one kid who didn't.
25. Talent got you noticed. It's the nights nobody clapped that got you good.
26. Stop shrinking the dream so it fits people who were never going to come anyway.
27. Falling for the wrong people taught you the exact shape of the right one.
28. You forgave them so many times you forgot to press charges on yourself for staying.
29. Peace isn't the absence of noise. It's no longer needing to be understood by people who won't.
30. You survived the thing you were sure would end you, and still ask permission to rest.
31. Growth is quiet. If everyone's clapping, check whether you actually moved.
32. You're not tired from doing too much. You're tired from carrying people who won't walk.
33. The apology you're still waiting for is rent on a house you don't even live in anymore.
34. Some people only love the version of you that never asks them for anything back.
35. You keep calling it a phase so you don't have to call it a pattern.
36. Discipline is just you keeping a promise on the day the feeling that made it is gone.
37. You don't need closure. You need to stop reopening the door to check if they came back.
38. The people clapping for your potential go quiet the second you actually use it.
39. You learned to be easy to leave. Unlearn it before it costs you the one who'd stay.
40. The loudest people in your life were never the ones who showed up when it went quiet.

## HOW TO USE THESE (for artists & content)
- Drop one as a caption before you post a track.
- Use as hook text on a reel / short (top 2 seconds = the line).
- Print on a wall, a tee, a note app — own it.
- The formula that tested fire: *You/I open → shape the feeling → punch.* 
  Accusatory beats poetic. Specific beats vague.

Own your voice. — nbskole
